About the Faculty

Our History

Established in 1963, the Faculty of Medicine at the University of Zimbabwe has been at the forefront of medical education and healthcare in Zimbabwe and the region.

Learn More

Our Mission

To provide quality medical education, conduct innovative research, and deliver excellent healthcare services to improve the health of communities in Zimbabwe and beyond.

Learn More

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in medical education, research, and healthcare delivery in Africa, recognized for innovation and impact.

Learn More
